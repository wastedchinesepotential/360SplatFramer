Supported: Pre-stitched Equirectangular .mp4 / .mov

Max Size: 500MB (Recommend trimming to 15-60 seconds for training)

How to prepare your footage:
  1. Open your `.insv` file in Insta360 Studio.
  2. Trim to exactly the 15-45 second clip you want to train on.
  3. In Export settings, select 360 Video (Equirectangular).
  4. Resolution: Max available (e.g., 5.7K or 8K).
  5. Format: `.mp4` (H.264 or H.265). Avoid ProRes as file sizes will be far too large for browser processing.
  6. Bitrate: Target 100-150 Mbps for high quality. Ensure the final exported file remains under 500MB.
